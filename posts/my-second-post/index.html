Explanation of Terms 名词解释

Information 信息

Information can be thought of as the resolution of uncertainty; it is that which answers the question of “What an entity is” and thus defines both its essence and nature of its characteristics. The concept of information has different meanings in different contexts.

信息可以被认为是不确定性的解决方案;它回答了“实体是什么”的问题，从而界定了实体的本质和其自然特性。信息的概念在不同的语境中有不同的含义。

Positional notation 进位制

Positional notation (or place-value notation, or positional numeral system) denotes usually the extension to any base of the Hindu Arabic numeral system (or decimal system). More generally, a positional system is a numeral system in which the contribution of a digit to the value of a number is the product of the value of the digit by a factor determined by the position of the digit.

进位制（或进位计数法或位值计数法）通常表示对印度－阿拉伯数字系统（或十进制）的任何基数的扩展。更一般地，进位制是一种数字系统，其中，数字对数字值的贡献是该数字的值与该数字的位置的乘积。

Algorithm 算法

In mathematics and computer science, an algorithm (/ˈælɡərɪðəm/) is a finite sequence of well-defined, computer-implementable instructions, typically to solve a class of problems or to perform a computation. Algorithms are always unambiguous and are used as specifications for performing calculations, data processing, automated reasoning, and other tasks.

在数学和计算机科学中，算法（/ælɡərɪðəm/）是定义明确的计算机可实现指令的有限序列，通常用于解决一类问题或执行计算。 算法始终是明确的，并被用作于执行计算，数据处理，自动推理和其他任务。

Software bug 程序错误

A software bug is an error, flaw or fault in a computer program or system that causes it to produce an incorrect or unexpected result, or to behave in unintended ways. The process of finding and fixing bugs is termed “debugging” and often uses formal techniques or tools to pinpoint bugs, and since the 1950s, some computer systems have been designed to also deter, detect or auto-correct various computer bugs during operations.

程序错误是指计算机程序或系统中的错误，缺陷或故障，导致其产生不正确或意外的结果，或行为异常。发现和修复错误的过程称为“调试”，通常使用正式的技术或工具来查明错误，并且自 1950 年代以来，某些计算机系统的设计目的还在于阻止，检测或自动纠正各种计算机错误。

Translation of concepts 写出以下概念的英文单词

十进制 Decimal

The decimal numeral system (also called base-ten positional numeral system, and occasionally called denary or decanary) is the standard system for denoting integer and non-integer numbers. It is the extension to non-integer numbers of the Hindu–Arabic numeral system. The way of denoting numbers in the decimal system is often referred to as decimal notation.

二进制 Binary

In mathematics and digital electronics, a binary number is a number expressed in the base-2 numeral system or binary numeral system, which uses only two symbols: typically “0” (zero) and “1” (one).

八进制 Octal

The octal numeral system, or oct for short, is the base-8 number system, and uses the digits 0 to 7. Octal numerals can be made from binary numerals by grouping consecutive binary digits into groups of three (starting from the right).

十六进制 Hexadecimal

In mathematics and computing, hexadecimal (also base 16, or hex) is a positional system that represents numbers using a base of 16. Unlike the common way of representing numbers with ten symbols, it uses sixteen distinct symbols, most often the symbols “0”–“9” to represent values zero to nine, and “A”–“F” (or alternatively “a”–“f”) to represent values ten to fifteen.
